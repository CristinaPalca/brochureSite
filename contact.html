ABOUT THE SHOP

Little King Goods is a small boutique located in Morriston, Ontario, Canada. LKG was founded in 2016 and is dedicated to produce the highest quality of handcrafted leather goods as possible.

All of our goods are crafted by a single craftsmen. We use ethically sourced materials and our goods are fair trade made.

We hope you enjoy your visit!
